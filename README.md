# BLAST · BLASTER MASTER

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **HOBBY**.

Blaster Master (Sunsoft, NES 1988) — a game-world, and Sunsoft's dual-mode classic: the localization of the Famicom's 超惑星戦記メタファイト (Chō Wakusei Senki Metafight, an interstellar planetary-war epic). The West bolted on a homier frame — a boy, JASON, whose pet frog FRED touches a radioactive chest, mutates, and falls down a hole; Jason follows and finds the battle tank SOPHIA THE 3RD. TWO games in one cartridge: side-scrolling caverns driving SOPHIA + top-down OVERHEAD dungeon-bases on foot with an upgradeable gun. A Metroid-like gated descent — grow the tank HOVER→DIVE→WALL-CLIMB until it drives on ceilings — eight areas down to the PLUTONIUM BOSS. THE NEW CANVAS-3D TITLE MODE: an animated software-rendered (no WebGL) rotating low-poly SOPHIA THE 3RD over a perspective cavern grid (depth-sorted, Lambert-shaded; paint-first) + the amber Sunsoft logo + a PUSH-START banner + 8-bit/CRT. Render-not-invent: the Metafight localization (hero Kane Gardner; 'Sophia the 3rd' was the PLANET's name, repurposed as the tank's), 'J-7' softened to later-series canon, the celebrated Naoki Kodaka score, the legacy (Blaster Master 2 / Blasting Again / Blaster Master Zero 2017 re-canon) all flagged. 10 emergents across all four natures — Jason · Fred · SOPHIA · the Upgrades · the Overhead · the Plutonium Boss · the Mutants · the Subterranean World · Metafight (the true self) · the Sunsoft Sound.
